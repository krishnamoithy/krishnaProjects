Module 5

Skills Gap Analysis

Skill gap analysis is a strategic tool that evaluates the disparity between current workforce capabilities and the skills required for optimal performance. This process identifies areas where employees lack essential skills, enabling organizations to implement targeted training and development initiatives, ensuring a skilled and proficient workforce aligned with business objectives.

In conclusion, skill gap analysis is pivotal for organizational growth. By pinpointing disparities between existing skills and those crucial for success, businesses can proactively address shortcomings. This strategic approach enhances employee performance, fosters professional development, and ultimately positions the organization to thrive in an ever-evolving and competitive landscape.
